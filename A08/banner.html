Pagtatag! is the second extended play (EP) recorded by the Filipino boy band SB19. Sony Music Philippines released it on June 9, 2023. The EP includes songs all co-written by the band's leader Pablo, which incorporated musical styles such as pop, EDM, hip hop, soul, and R&B, as part of the band's desire to explore genres since their departure from the dance-pop music in their debut album, Get in the Zone (2020).
